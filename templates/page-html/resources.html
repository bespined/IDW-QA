Student Support Resources

Your success matters. These resources are here to help you thrive in this course and at ASU.

Accessibility & Disability Resources

If you have a disability or condition that may affect your ability to participate in this course, accommodations are available. Register early to ensure timely support.

  • Student Accessibility and Inclusive Learning Services (SAILS) (opens in new tab) — Register for accommodations, request exam adjustments, connect with an access consultant
  • Request Accommodations (opens in new tab) — Start the accommodation process online

Technical Support

  • Canvas Support (24/7) (opens in new tab) — Help with Canvas access, submissions, quizzes, and technical issues
  • ASU Help Center (opens in new tab) — General ASU technology support (ASURITE, email, software)

Academic Support

  • ASU Tutoring (opens in new tab) — Free tutoring for many subjects, including science and math
  • ASU Library Resources (opens in new tab) — Research databases, librarian consultations, study spaces
  • Success Coaching (opens in new tab) — One-on-one coaching for time management, study strategies, and goal setting

Academic Advising

  • eAdvisor (opens in new tab) — Degree progress tracking and academic planning
  • Find Your Academic Advisor (opens in new tab) — Connect with your college or department advisor

Wellness & Personal Support

  • Counseling Services (opens in new tab) — Free, confidential counseling for ASU students
  • Crisis Line: Call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, available 24/7)
  • Dean of Students (opens in new tab) — Support for personal emergencies, academic disruptions, and crisis situations

Need Help in This Course?

Your instructor is here to support your learning. Reach out anytime via Canvas Inbox — you can expect a response within 24 hours on weekdays. Check the syllabus for office hours and additional contact information.
