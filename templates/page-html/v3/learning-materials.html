Required Readings

Chapter 1: Introduction to Human Factors & Human Performance

Wickens, Hollands, Banbury & Parasuraman — Engineering Psychology and Human Performance, 4th or 5th ed.

Covers the discipline of human factors, models of human performance, error taxonomies (slips, lapses, mistakes), and why systemic analysis outperforms individual blame. Pay close attention to the section on error classification — you will reference this framework throughout the course.

  • Access via your required course textbook.

Chapter 2: Signal Detection Theory

Wickens, Hollands, Banbury & Parasuraman — Engineering Psychology and Human Performance, 4th or 5th ed.

The core SDT chapter. Focus on the ROC curve, d-prime (d'), and the concept of criterion (β). You do not need to memorize formulas — understand the conceptual relationships. The quiz and assignment test application, not calculation.

  • Access via your required course textbook.
